# Competitive Effects of Spirit Airlines' Bankruptcy on U.S. Domestic Airfares

**Aryan Goel** | University of Chicago | May 2026

Economic consulting work sample analyzing the fare effects of Spirit Airlines' bankruptcy and route exits using BTS DB1B data (2022-2025). Difference-in-differences framework with route and quarter fixed effects.

## Data
- Primary: BTS Airline Origin and Destination Survey (DB1B), 2022Q1-2025Q2
- 57 million ticket observations cleaned to 176,405 route-carrier-quarter aggregates

## Key Finding
Fares on Spirit-exited routes rose approximately 1.3% relative to control routes after bankruptcy, but the effect is not statistically significant. Competitive backfill by Delta (+15.3%), American (+16.3%), and Southwest (+13.2%) largely maintained competition on exited routes.
